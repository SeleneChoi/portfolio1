SELENE CHOI.

I'm a Vancouver-based front-end web developer with a specialization in UX/UI design, creating visually stunning and intuitive websites that seamlessly blend front-end expertise with user psychology.

Collaboration is at the core of my approach, as I thrive in team environments, working closely with designers, developers, and stakeholders to bring concepts to life. I prioritize user research and feedback, making them integral to my design process, thus ensuring the end product perfectly caters to the target audience's needs.

What sets me apart is my unique background in the medical industry as a registered nurse, which grants me a profound understanding of communication and psychology. This knowledge, combined with the inspiration drawn from Vancouver's vibrant culture and breathtaking landscapes, fuels my creativity and drives my work forward.
