Company Overview

Meeple 4 People (MP Cafe). An entertainment cafe with a board game theme located in downtown Vancouver, B.C. The cafe offers a variety of board games for all players. Our goal is to create a place for people to disconnect from the online world and socialize with friends and family through fun games and food.

  • Board games are for pre-teen to young adivts (e.g., cards, dice base, team player, strategy games, etc. No digital games)
  • Food includes drinks, snacks, and light meals (e.g., tea/coffee, cookies, popcorn, wraps, sandwiches, etc.)
  • MP Cafe features a simple cozy design to set a relaxing environment for the best board game experience

    • Website Purpose

    The purpose of our website is to provide current and prospective customers with a user-friendly interface that makes it easy to find the information they are looking for. Some of the content they will be able to find from our site includes:

  • Feature game | snacks
  • Search facets | filters
  • Reservations for events
  • Contact Page | Location map
  • Social Media sharing
  • About us page on the company and its community
