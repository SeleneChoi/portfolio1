Overview

Portfolio Version 1 reflects my initial venture into web portfolios, drawing inspiration from classic business cards. I aimed to harmonize modern digital design with timeless simplicity, addressing the user's preference for straightforward, efficient access to information. My primary objective was a design that emphasizes simplicity and responsiveness, surpassing dark themes and jQuery plugins to ensure effortless user engagement.

Design Process

  • Ideation

    I prefer to begin with a sketch, whether it's on my iPad or in a notebook. During this phase, I aim to limit the number of colors to three, striving for a simple and functional aesthetic. To enhance the resemblance to physical postcards or business cards, I've added a frame to the top left and bottom right corners.

  • Prototyping

    I've developed a dark theme to evaluate its appearance. As illustrated below, I contemplated two options for the navigation bar: a single expanded line and a corner frame. In the end, I chose the expanded line to achieve a more physical card-like appearance.
